## The problem

Most enterprises now run AI agents in production, but many lack **formal, request-level governance**. Static policies checked at session start are no longer enough — boards and regulators want proof on **every material action**.

| What buyers feel today | Why native tooling falls short |
|------------------------|--------------------------------|
| “Copilot is live — who approved what?” | Audit logs show activity, not **admissibility** |
| Legal updated policy — agents still act on old rules | **Governance latency** measured in quarters |
| Board asks for evidence before the next meeting | Assessments ≠ ongoing **signed receipts** |

Noetfield closes the gap: **evaluate → sign → log** before execution, with **instant re-brief** when policy changes.
