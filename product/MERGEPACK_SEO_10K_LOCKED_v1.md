## 3. Funnel (3 layers)

### Layer 1 — SEO landing (capture)

- Path pattern: `/merge-pdf`, `/combine-pdf`, `/merge-pdf-without-watermark`, …
- Above fold: upload → merge → download (**&lt;2s to action**)
- No signup on first merge

### Layer 2 — monetization

| Tier | Rules |
|------|--------|
| **Free** | 3 merges/day · size cap · optional 3s delay OR small footer (A/B) |
| **Sub $9–12/mo** | Unlimited · batch · no footer · priority queue |
| **Day pass $2** | Urgent one-off (closings, applications) |

### Layer 3 — trust (conversion multiplier)

- Files deleted within **1 hour** (automated cleanup job v1.1)
- Privacy page plain English
- HTTPS · no account for free
- “We don’t sell files” one-liner on upload screen
