## How smart startups sit in chains (June 2026)

Vendors do not usually pay cash for “being a customer.” They pay **distribution, proof, and consumption**:

| Chain seat | What you give them | What you get |
|------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Build partner** | Product built on their API (Cursor → Anthropic) | Credits, rate limits, co-marketing |
| **Reference architecture** | Case study in their category | Credits, conference, solutions engineer |
| **Startup program** | Logo + usage growth trajectory | $2.5K–$350K credits (stacked) |
| **ISV / marketplace** | Sell into their enterprise buyers | Rev share, leads, Copilot/Azure listing |
| **Governance complement** | Fill gap they don’t ship (HITL, receipts) | OSS co-build, Azure co-sell |

**Your wedge:** SourceA is **controlled consumption + audit receipts** — partners want customers who **use API at scale with compliance story**. Noetfield/TrustField are **invoice surfaces**; SourceA is **proof factory**.
