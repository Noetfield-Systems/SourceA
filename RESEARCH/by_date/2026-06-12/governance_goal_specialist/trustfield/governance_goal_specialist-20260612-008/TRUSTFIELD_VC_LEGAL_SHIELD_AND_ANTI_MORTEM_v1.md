## Executive summary

**TrustField** is your **credibility surface** — the company VCs, MSBs, and regulated buyers meet first. **SourceA** is the engine they never need to understand on day one. **Noetfield** is the governance product narrative; **TrustField** is where **money, contracts, and compliance reputation** live.

Your ask: protect **intent, inventions, brand, and credibility from day one** — and build a platform so good that **VCs trust the relationship before they trust the pitch**.

**Core strategy:**

1. **Legal shield first** (Phase 0) — ownership, confidentiality, representations, MSB truth-in-advertising  
2. **Proof second** (Phase 1) — live tamper demo + receipt export (ENFORCEMENT-6MO)  
3. **Relationship third** (Phase 2) — VC-grade data room + one paying pilot  
4. **Capital fourth** (Phase 3+) — seed on proof, not manifesto  

**One sentence for TrustField external:**

> TrustField Technologies helps regulated organizations prove that AI and payment operations ran under enforceable governance — with receipts, not dashboards.

## Part 6 — TrustField as VC relationship platform

### What “platform so good VCs visit” means

Not a content farm. A **structured diligence room**:

| Zone | Contents | Update cadence |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **A — Thesis** | 3-slide deck · category sentence · problem | Monthly |
| **B — Proof** | Demo video · validator output · redacted receipts | After each demo release |
| **C — Commercial** | Anonymized SOW · pipeline table (honest) | After W3 |
| **D — Legal** | Corp docs · IP assignment · templates (counsel) | Phase 0 + pre-seed |
| **E — Technical appendix** | Architecture constellation · ENFORCEMENT index | On DD request |
| **F — Risk** | Pre-mortem + mitigations · incidents closed | Quarterly |

**Hosting:** Start `trustfield.ca/investors` password-protected or DocSend — **not** public until Phase 2.

### VC comfort checklist (first meeting)

- [ ] One sentence — no jargon sprint  
- [ ] Live or recorded demo < 6 min  
- [ ] Clear: **what customer pays** · **what you do NOT do** (hold funds, legal filing)  
- [ ] Honest stage: pre-revenue / pilot  
- [ ] Data room link ready  
- [ ] NDA offered if sharing export bundle  
- [ ] Ask: “What would kill this deal for you?” — write answer down
