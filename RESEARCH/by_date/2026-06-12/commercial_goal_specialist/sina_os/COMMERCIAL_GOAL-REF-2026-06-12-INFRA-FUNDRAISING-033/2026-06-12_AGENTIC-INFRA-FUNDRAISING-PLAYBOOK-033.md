## Part 3 — Did anyone raise "easily" via website alone?

**Short answer: No — not in agentic governance infra in 2026.**

What looks like "website → raise" is usually:

```text
Stealth build (6–18 mo) OR prior company exit
  → founder/operator signal queued
    → landing + manifesto + architecture
      → PR / launch day
        → lead VC already warm OR pilot LOI in pocket
          → round closes
```

**Vybe** had a public site and waitlist — but also **YC batch**, **First Round lead**, and founders with **$5M ARR prior company** story.

**Jetty** announced raise **same day** as PR — but had **Mila Ventures**, **AQC**, operators from Google/Meta AI, and **audit-trail product narrative** already built.

**Your honest path:** Website accelerates **inbound** after W1 demo + one pilot signal — not before.
