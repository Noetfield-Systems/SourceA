## Part II — Deep site-by-site analysis

### 1. Vanta (vanta.com) — **Primary reference for TrustField**

**What they do:** SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, PCI, GDPR — compliance automation with "Agentic Trust Platform" positioning.

**Homepage mechanics (live fetch):**
- Social proof immediately: "Trusted by 16,000+ customers"
- Quantified outcomes: "2,000 hrs saved," "20% faster deal cycles," "Automated 93% of questionnaires"
- Product pillars visible above fold: Compliance · Risk · Third Party Risk · Audit · Trust Center · Questionnaire Automation
- Framework grid (SOC 2, ISO 27001, **NIST AI RMF**, **ISO 42001**) — directly relevant to Noetfield cross-sell
- CTA: "GET COMPLIANT" — action verb tied to buyer outcome

**Steal for TrustField:**
| Vanta element | TrustField translation |
|---------------|------------------------|
| Fear → proof loop | "When FINTRAC asks…" → evidence pack sample |
| Trust Center | Static "Evidence Preview" page (pilot deliverables mock) |
| Framework logos | RPAA · FINTRAC MSB · PCMLTFA references (not fake certifications) |
| Quantified outcomes | "6-week pilot · 3 audit-ready artifact sets" (honest, not inflated) |
| Continuous monitoring | TF-P5 CCO Retainer = Drata-style "always on" upsell |

**Do not steal:** Enterprise pricing complexity · 16K customer claim · AI agent hype before you have live product
