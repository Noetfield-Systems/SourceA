Who this case study is for

Local trades and service operators — pool, spa, cleaning, maintenance — who need more contracts and documented visits.

Revenue agencies delivering acquisition systems for SMB clients with proof attached.

Not for: SaaS category-scale GEO buyers (see AgentGo #2).

The challenge

Local service businesses lose deals when prospects cannot see professionalism, pricing clarity, or proof of work. This operator competed against “the usual guy” with no documented visits and weak online presence.

  • Homeowners unsure service actually happened
  • Unclear tiers and weak differentiation
  • Leads not converting to annual plans

What shipped

A full acquisition system — not a brochure

Offer & positioning

Founding 25 annual offer, Vancouver-specific story, fixed tiers from $449/mo — comparison table vs typical service.

Lead capture

Booking intake with 4-hour confirmation promise · gate-code friendly · mobile-first layout.

PureFlow Report
