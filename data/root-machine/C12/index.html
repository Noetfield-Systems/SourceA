Frequently Asked Questions

How can I detect risky AI agent behavior in real time?

SourceA monitors agent activity at runtime, breaking interactions into granular steps and flagging suspicious behavior including prompt injection, data leaks, and over-permissioned actions.

Can I prevent prompt injection attacks in production?

Yes. AIDR identifies direct and indirect prompt injection, mapping to OWASP and MITRE ATLAS. Block or respond via policies or APIs.

How do I audit who's building and running AI agents?

Full inventory and attribution across platforms — who created them, what tools they use, and runtime activity to uncover shadow AI.

Can SourceA help secure sensitive data handled by copilots?

Identify when agents access PHI, PII, PCI, or secrets — flag, redact, or block before unsafe behavior spreads.
