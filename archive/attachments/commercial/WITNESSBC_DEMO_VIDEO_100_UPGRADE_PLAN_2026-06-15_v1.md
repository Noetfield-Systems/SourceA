## 1. Executive summary

**Why weak proof kills conversion.** In June 2026, enterprise buyers evaluating agentic governance have seen dozens of “AI security” homepages with stock motion, unverifiable stat strips, and demo videos that could have been recorded against mock data. GRC and platform engineering buyers treat unverifiable demos as marketing theater — they defer to vendors who show **policy-before-action**, **signed receipts**, and **tamper-evident replay** in the first 90 seconds. Vidyard’s 2025 B2B video benchmark (≈1M videos) still shows **65%+ completion only under one minute**; completion drops sharply after 2–3 minutes unless the viewer is already in a decision-stage evaluation.

**What professional models do differently.**

| Weak demo | Pro model |
|-----------|-----------|
| Slides + voiceover | Live terminal / real UI capture |
| “Trust us” metrics | Verifiable artifact (hash, receipt, replay) |
| One long tour | Modular clips by funnel stage (72s / 15s / 3min) |
| Book-a-demo only CTA | Proof Lab self-serve + optional human gate |
| Static MP4 on homepage | Interactive replay + deep links (`#scenario=outbound`) |
| No tamper story | Deliberate tamper-FAIL moment (signature invalid) |

WitnessBC’s differentiation is **human-in-the-loop civic/commercial workflow + cryptographic proof on disk** — not another AI firewall pitch. Films must show the six `proof-scenarios-v1.json` scenarios, bridge hero → Proof Lab with zero friction, and never conflate WitnessBC with WitnessAI (`witness.ai`).
