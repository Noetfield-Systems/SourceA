## Scoring rubric (0–100, W1-specific)

| Criterion | Weight | What “100” means |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| **Live terminal fidelity** | 30 | Records real CLI output; no fake UI generation |
| **Infra/SaaS polish** | 20 | Auto-zoom, cursor smooth, dev-tool aesthetic |
| **Speed to publish** | 15 | First Tier-1 export < half day solo founder |
| **Edit control** | 15 | Trim beats, captions, retake single beat |
| **W1 beat fit** | 10 | BLOCK/ALLOW/tamper timing + readability |
| **Cost (one demo)** | 5 | <$100 for one finished film |
| **Solo founder** | 5 | No agency, minimal learning curve |

**Tier bands:** T1 ≥85 · T2 70–84 · T3 55–69 · T4 <55 for W1
