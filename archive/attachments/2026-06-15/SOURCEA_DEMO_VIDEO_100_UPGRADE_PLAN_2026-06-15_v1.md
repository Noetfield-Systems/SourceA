## 1. Executive summary

**Market reality (June 2026):** B2B demo tooling has split into four lanes — (1) **polished screen capture** (Screen Studio, FocuSee, Tella), (2) **transcript-first edit** (Descript, ngram), (3) **interactive click-through** (Arcade, Navattic, Storylane, Walnut, Supademo), (4) **AI avatar/script** (HeyGen, Synthesia, Colossyan). Professional SaaS teams **stack 2–3 tools** by workflow: Screen Studio for hero films, Arcade/Navattic for website embed, Descript for sales cuts, Wistia/Vidyard for completion analytics.

**Completion data (honest):** Under **60 seconds** retains ~71% viewers; past **2 minutes** drops below ~56% for cold traffic (Demosmith · Wistia-adjacent benchmarks cited in 2026 playbooks). Enterprise decision-stage demos may run 3–5 minutes — but only after qualification.

**What winners do (steal these):**

| Model | Pattern | Steal for SourceA |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| **Linear** | Product speaks · keyboard speed · no preamble | W1 ALLOW beat — zero narration warmup |
| **Notion** | Simple → complex evolution on one surface | Home command center tab rotation |
| **Stripe** | Infrastructure clarity · compound value | Proof chain row · API hook on platform page |
| **Arcade** | One capture → video + interactive + sales URL | AEG page + eval pack + embed |
| **Navattic** | Pixel-perfect HTML capture for technical buyers | Platform/security procurement path |
| **Datadog** | Stat-led urgency · keynote clip discipline | Receipt ticker · Valid YES strip |
| **ProPublica** | Methodology page + reproducible data | `proof/live.html` + PDF bundle |

**SourceA honest gap today:** Interactive W1 player exists on `proof.html#w1-demo-film` but **no shipped MP4 take**; outbound still links ephemeral trycloudflare; completion metrics not wired. Gate J (W1 film receipt) and Gate K (durable URL) block "pro-grade" public proof.

|--------------|--------|--------|---------------|-----|

| Sales 1:1 | **2–3m** | Vidyard personalized | Eval pack + prospect name | Reply with two times |
| PLG self-serve | **Interactive** | Arcade/Navattic embed | AEG + command center tabs | Try sourcea-boot |

**Script law (PAS / 3-act):**

- **Act 1 (10%):** Buyer pain in their words — "Can you prove what ran last night?"
- **Act 2 (70%):** Real product — boot gate · ALLOW · BLOCK · receipt on disk
- **Act 3 (20%):** Quantified outcome + **one CTA**

**Pacing:** 150–170 words/min narration · no shot >8s without cursor motion or zoom.

## 10. Lessons learned (research)

1. **2026 market is workflow-split** — capture ≠ interactive ≠ avatar; pro teams combine.
