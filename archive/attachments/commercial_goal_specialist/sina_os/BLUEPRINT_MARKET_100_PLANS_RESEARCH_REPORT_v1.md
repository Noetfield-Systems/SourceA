- **Penalties:** up to €35M / 7% turnover (serious); €15M / 3% documentation gaps
- **78%** enterprises unprepared (Vision Compliance via Optro)
- **Procurement:** 8–10 week integrated playbook; **30-day PoC** parallel to RFP ([AgentMode](https://agentmodeai.com/enterprise-agentic-ai-procurement-playbook/), [AI Compliance RFP](https://aicompliancevendors.com/guides/ai-compliance-rfp-template))

- **No federal AIDA** (Bill C-27 dead Jan 2025) — patchwork: **PIPEDA** + **Quebec Law 25** (use as national floor) + sector (OSFI) ([AIRiskAware](https://www.airiskaware.com/insights/canada-ai-governance-no-federal-law))
