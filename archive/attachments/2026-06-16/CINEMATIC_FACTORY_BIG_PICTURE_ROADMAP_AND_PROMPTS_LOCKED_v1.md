```mermaid
  REF[References locked] --> REC[Record Screen Studio]

  ING --> CRIT[Critic vs pick ID]
  CRIT -->|PASS| SHIP[Ship gate publish_allowed]
  CRIT -->|BLOCK| LEARN[Learn row + rule patch]
  LEARN --> REC
  SHIP --> MEM[Memory incident]
  MEM --> REF
```

- Critic compares to **STYLE-A3 / B1 / A5** — not internal A+.
- BLOCK without learn row = incomplete loop.
