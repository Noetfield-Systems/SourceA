## Three layers (order matters)

| Step | Layer | Output |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1 | **Translate** | Founder intent in plain words |
| 2 | **Map** | One authority row or `*_LOCKED` path |
| 3 | **Machine** | Task · gate · one proof command |

Never skip step 1 for cold machine-only replies on governance topics.

## When to run (mandatory)

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|

| User: compare Brain vs megachat / maintainer | Two-brain table in bridge law §1 |
