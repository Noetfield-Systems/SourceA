## C) Product Strategy Upgrade (Delivery + Trust)

- One global rule: every lane must produce a **proof packet id** before commercial handoff.
- Before: CTAs and support text were mixed; proof could be accessed later.
- After: intake emits packet seed; execution emits receipt, audit tag, decision log, replay; offer surfaces require packet token.
- Trust chain per step: intake hash -> execution receipt -> policy audit tag -> replay -> offer.
