## Executive summary (read this first)

SourceA is **not** a compliance SaaS company yet. It is a **Governed Execution OS** — governance on disk, validators as truth, workers as execution, receipts as proof. That makes it closer to **Credo AI + Vanta + Linear + Resend** combined at the *philosophy* layer, but closer to **Supabase/Stripe** at the *buyer* layer (developers and technical founders who need infrastructure they can trust).

The 10 sites share one pattern the founder paste got exactly right:

> **Lead with the customer's fear or job, show the product immediately, one primary CTA, design signals seriousness and nothing-to-hide.**

SourceA's golden move is **not** to become "Vanta for AI agents" on day one. It is to **productize the receipt layer you already built** — the thing no competitor shows on their homepage because most AI tools still treat chat as memory.

**One-line positioning (recommended):**

> **Prove every agent action — before the model runs, after it ships.**

**Three-word category (recommended):**

> **Agentic trust infrastructure**

**What to sell first (aligned with Goal Hierarchy T0 + T2 FORGE):**

| Surface | Buyer | Proof |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **FORGE** (T2 primary) | Founders / small teams | "Ship an app with governed agents" |
| **SourceA spine** (T0) | Technical buyers / design partners | Eval-1b + dispatch gate + receipt API |
| **Portfolio OS** (investor) | Angels / strategics | Multi-business founder operating at scale |

**What to hide from default homepage:** portfolio company logos, MSB/TrustField, RunReceipt factory, five-entity complexity.
