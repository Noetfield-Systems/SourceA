## The opportunity

Enterprises and founders need **one governed stack** — not ten AI tools. They need:

- A credible **website** (MVP → production)
- **Agentic workflows** (mail, ads, ops, support) with human-in-the-loop
- **Governance** (audit receipts, no silent automation, role boundaries)
- **One team** to run it — not a fleet of freelancers

SourceA already built the hard part internally: layer stack, cloud factory, receipt-native drains, portfolio lanes (TrustField · Noetfield · WitnessBC). **Productize that as a service.**
