## 1. What “good 2-tier” means in 2026

| Pattern | Tier 1 (land) | Tier 2 (expand) | Who does it well |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Hybrid SaaS** | Free / Hobby $0–$29 | Pro $50–$199 + usage | Trigger.dev, Inngest, Langfuse, Zapier |
| **Agency wedge** | Diagnostic $1.5K–$5K | Build $8K–$25K + retainer $3K–$8K/mo | Pickaxe-style AI agencies, Noetfield Intelligence 613 |
| **Outcome** | Pilot / shadow | Per-resolution or per-meeting | Intercom Fin, Zendesk AI, 11x |
| **Compliance** | Readiness scan | Continuous control + audit export | Vanta, Drata, Secureframe |
| **Vertical SaaS** | $99–$199/mo niche tool | $499/mo + services | Vertical agents (2–3× premium vs horizontal) |

**2026 shift:** Per-seat alone is dying for autonomous agents. **43% of SaaS** already uses hybrid (base + usage); heading to **~61%** by late 2026. Price at **30–50% of manual cost** replaced, not cost-plus.
